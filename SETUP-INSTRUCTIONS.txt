REMI WINTERS WEBSITE - SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
==========================================

1. ADD YOUR IMAGES
------------------
Drop these files into the "images/" folder with these EXACT names:

  remi-logo.png          - Your Remi Winters logo (the red lips/knife one)
  tmchftlk-cover.jpg     - Trading My Cheating Husband cover
  his-hostage-cover.jpg  - His Hostage cover
  dirty-daddies-cover.jpg - Dirty Daddies cover (either version)
  dirty-daddies-bonus-cover.jpg - Dirty Daddies Bonus cover
  dead-biker-cover.jpg   - How to F*ck a Dead Biker cover

(JPG or PNG both work - just match the filename)


2. DEPLOY ON VERCEL (replace your current site)
------------------------------------------------
Option A: Drag & drop the whole "remi-winters-site" folder to vercel.com/new
Option B: Push to GitHub and connect to Vercel

To get a custom domain (recommended):
- Buy remiwinters.com (~$12/year from Namecheap, Google Domains, etc.)
- In Vercel dashboard > Settings > Domains > Add your domain
- Follow the DNS instructions they give you


3. CONNECT MAILING LIST (MailerLite)
-------------------------------------
- Sign up free at mailerlite.com
- Create a subscriber group called "Remi Winters Readers"
- Go to Forms > Embedded Forms > Create
- Copy the form action URL
- In index.html, find the <form> tag and replace action="#" with your MailerLite form URL
- Change method to match MailerLite's instructions


4. UPDATE YOUR AMAZON AUTHOR PAGE LINK
---------------------------------------
In the footer, update the Amazon link to your actual author page URL.


That's it! The site is fully functional without images too -
it uses styled placeholders that still look good.

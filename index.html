About Remi

I write the books your mother warned you about. Paranormal romance, contemporary heat, dark captive fantasies, and the kind of spicy that makes you check over your shoulder before turning the page.

My men are possessive, unhinged, and would commit several felonies for the right woman. My women are sharp, messy, and tired of settling. If you like your romance served with a side of unbridled spice, content warnings, and zero apologies — you're home.

I believe women shamelessly embracing smut is political. I will die on this hill. Preferably surrounded by fictional men who are obsessed with me.

Currently writing: the Fated to the Lycan King series. Currently surviving on: caffeine and spite.
